Lisbon Macro Workshop

September 2-3, 2022

Call for papers

The Lisbon Macro Workshop will run from the morning of Friday, September 2, to lunchtime on Saturday, September 3, at Católica Lisbon School of Business & Economics.

Papers can be submitted until May 15 to lisbonmacro@gmail.com. We are particularly interested in papers aimed at understanding firm behavior and its relevance for macro questions, but any macro paper will be considered. In your submission please indicate whether you would be interested in participating as a discussant if your paper can’t be accommodated in the program. Decisions will be communicated by June 15.

Participants will need to cover their own travel and accommodation, and there will be a registration fee of approximately €80 to cover meals and catering during the conference.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at the above email address. We look forward to seeing you in Lisbon!

Organizers: Nicholas Kozeniauskas and Laszlo Tetenyi
